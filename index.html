Matthew DiGiuseppe

Associate Professor of International Relations

Institute of Political Science, Leiden University

Download CV Google Scholar

My research and teaching interests include international and comparative political economy, armed conflict, interstate alliances and the domestic roots of foreign policy. I am currently the PI on the Microfoundations of Debt Crisis (MIDEBT) project that is funded by an ERC Starting Grant.

Research

Current Project

Microfoundations of Debt Crisis (MIDEBT)

Funded by an ERC Starting Grant

This project investigates how the public thinks about public debt and how that influences the accumulation of public debt and the reluctance to reduce it.
